Start by uploading the 'woocommerce' folder from the item pack into your theme folder. e.g. 'yourtheme/woocommerce'.

To update the colors,  in your Wordpress Dashboard, go to WooCommerce > Settings and click the 'Emails' tab. Use the Color fields to update the templates theme. Paste your logo URL to the  'Header Image' field, to add a image-based logo (max width 144px).

* Default Colors 

Base Colour: #c4ae85
Background Colour: #f5f4f0
Email Body Background Colour: #ffffff
Email Body Text Colour: #828688
